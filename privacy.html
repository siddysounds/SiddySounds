Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Siddy Sounds. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share your information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: Information you provide, such as your name, email address, and interactions with our website.
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected data, including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs.
  • Cookies & Tracking Data: Data collected through cookies and tracking technologies such as Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and improve our services
  • To analyze website usage and optimize user experience
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Your consent (where applicable)
  • Contractual necessity (to provide our services)
  • Legitimate interests (such as improving our website)
  • Legal compliance (when required by law)

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies, including:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity).
  • Advertising Cookies: May be used for personalized ads (if applicable).

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We may share limited data with trusted third-party services to operate our website, such as:

Google Analytics

Google Analytics helps us analyze website traffic. Learn more in their Privacy Policy.

Microsoft Clarity

Microsoft Clarity tracks user interactions, including:

  • Mouse movements, clicks, and scrolling behavior
  • Session recordings and heatmaps
  • Technical details (browser type, operating system, screen resolution)

By using our website, you consent to this tracking. Learn more in the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You acknowledge and accept the risks of using our services.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • The right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • The right to withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • The right to restrict processing or object to data use
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to file a complaint with a regulatory authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at mail@siddysounds.com.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country, subject to appropriate safeguards, in compliance with relevant data protection laws.

Retention of Data

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for legal, contractual, or operational reasons.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of our website signifies acceptance of the latest version.

Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at mail@siddysounds.com.
