Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Siddy Sounds. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any information you provide while using our website.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require users to register or create accounts. However, we may collect limited personal data when you voluntarily contact us via email.

  • Email Address: If you send us an email, we receive your email address and any information you include in the message.
  • Technical Data: We use analytics services (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity) to collect non-personal information such as browser type, device, and time spent on our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information you provide is used only for the purpose of responding to your inquiries or improving our website. Specifically:

  • Email Communication: If you contact us, we will use your email address only to respond. We do not add it to any mailing lists or share it with third parties.
  • Analytics: We use tracking tools to improve user experience (see Section 4).

4. Analytics and Tracking

We use third-party analytics services to understand how visitors interact with our website. These services collect non-personal data such as:

  • Browser type, device type
  • Time spent on pages
  • Clicks, scrolls, and user interactions
  • Referring website

The services we use include:

  • Google Analytics: Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity: Privacy Policy

You can disable tracking by adjusting your browser settings or using Google’s opt-out tools.

5. Email Security

We do not guarantee the security of emails sent over the internet. If you send us an email, you acknowledge that:

  • Your email is transmitted through third-party services (e.g., Gmail, Outlook, or your email provider).
  • Emails are not always encrypted and could be intercepted by unauthorized parties.
  • We do not store your email beyond what is necessary to respond to your inquiry.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve functionality and for analytics tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services & External Links

We are not responsible for third-party services linked from our website, including email providers, analytics platforms, or external links. Please review their respective privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information on how your data is used.
  • Request deletion of any data you have voluntarily provided.

To exercise these rights, contact us at mail@siddysounds.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of our website means acceptance of the latest version.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at mail@siddysounds.com.
