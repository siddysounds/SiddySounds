Cookies Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Siddy Sounds. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Enhance website functionality and security.
  • Analyze traffic and improve user experience.
  • Personalize content and remember user preferences.

This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies and how you can manage your preferences.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. These files help improve website performance and user experience by:

  • Remembering login details and user preferences.
  • Tracking website performance and analyzing visitor behavior.
  • Providing personalized content and relevant ads.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies for various purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality, including security and accessibility.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Help analyze site usage and improve performance (e.g., Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity).
  • Functionality Cookies: Store user preferences such as language, theme settings, and login details.
  • Advertising & Marketing Cookies: Used for personalized advertising and tracking browsing behavior.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may place cookies on your device. These include:

Google Analytics

Google Analytics helps us track visitor behavior and improve website performance. Read more in their Privacy Policy.

Microsoft Clarity

Microsoft Clarity records user interactions, such as:

  • Mouse movements, clicks, and scrolling behavior.
  • Session recordings and heatmaps.
  • Technical data (browser type, OS, screen resolution).

By using our website, you consent to this tracking. Read more in the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage and disable cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Apple Safari

Note: Disabling cookies may impact website functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent for cookies at any time.
  • Request access to any collected data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.

To exercise your rights, contact us at mail@siddysounds.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy periodically. Continued use of our site means acceptance of the latest version.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at mail@siddysounds.com.
