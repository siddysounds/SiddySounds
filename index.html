About me

Siddy is a dynamic creator who fuses his tech expertise with an undeniable passion for music. By day, he navigates the world of IT, but by night, he immerses himself in the art of crafting beats, writing lyrics, and experimenting with fresh sounds. Through his music, Siddy pours his heart out, aiming to inspire, uplift, and share positive energy with every listener. His journey is all about blending technology and music to tell stories and create meaningful connections. Come along for the ride as he unveils his creative process and the music that drives his soul.
